OUR DAYS OF ACTIVITIES

Sunday Worship Service

A Bible-based word service organized by DLCF ABSU every Sunday providing spiritual guidance, teaching, and fellowship at 8am.

Monday Bible Study

Weekly Bible study every Monday at 6pm. Students gather to study God’s Word, pray, and grow spiritually through discussions and teachings.

Thursday Revival Hour

A refreshing service on Thursdays at 6pm. Students pray, worship, and receive inspiration to live a godly life in Christ.

Saturday Service & Fellowship

A fellowship gathering for all church workers to engage in discussions, and listening to message that shapens them for the work in God's vineyard.

Our Location

DLCF ABSU is located at the Public Health block, 200 level classrooms. Join us for our worship services and activities.

DLCF ABSU

Deeper Life Campus Fellowship (DLCF) ABSU nurtures students spiritually, academically, and morally, creating leaders who impact their campus and community positively.
