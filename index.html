Pastor (Dr.) W.F. Kumuyi, the General Superintendent of Deeper Christian Life Ministry, has devoted over four decades to advancing the kingdom of God globally. His life is characterized by unyielding commitment to holiness, spiritual discipline, and rigorous study of the Word of God.
